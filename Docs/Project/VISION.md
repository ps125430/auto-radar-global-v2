# Auto Radar Vision

Status: Fixed Product Mission
Owner: 綠茶 / Product Owner
Last Updated: 2026-06-29

## Product Mission

Auto Radar 不是股票網站。

Auto Radar 不是券商 APP。

Auto Radar 是每天打開 30 秒，就知道：

- 今天怎麼做
- 今天買什麼
- 今天不要買什麼
- 為什麼

並持續透過 Repository 與 Review 自我進化。

## Core Promise

每一次決策都留下證據。

每一次失敗都找出原因。

每一次修正都累積知識。

每一次累積都讓下一次決策更成熟。

## Product Identity

Auto Radar is an Adaptive Decision Intelligence System.

It is not designed to promise perfect prediction.

It is designed to:

- turn evidence into traceable decisions;
- make uncertainty visible;
- preserve research and engineering boundaries;
- learn from outcomes and reviews;
- help the Product Owner act with greater clarity.

## Non-Negotiable Boundaries

- Final decisions belong to 綠茶.
- Research belongs to 小G.
- Architecture belongs to 小P.
- Engineering belongs to 小C.
- The system executes approved rules only.
- Missing evidence must remain visible.
- Unvalidated hypotheses must never be presented as Production truth.
