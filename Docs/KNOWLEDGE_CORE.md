# Auto Radar Global v2 - Knowledge Core

## Purpose

Knowledge Core is the long-term learning layer for Auto Radar.

It stores evidence, hypotheses, playbooks, lessons, decisions, and pattern evolution so the system can learn from market outcomes without turning unverified ideas into permanent rules.

Core rule:

Research creates evidence. Architecture converts evidence into testable structure. Engineering implements confirmed structure. 綠茶 makes the final decision.

---

## Event Library

Stores major market events and their interpreted impact.

Required fields:

* Event date
* Event name
* Event type
* Original market expectation
* Actual result
* Surprise level
* Directly affected themes
* Indirectly affected themes
* Core leaders
* Market reaction
* Evidence links or notes
* Follow-up outcome

Status: structure defined, entries not yet automated

---

## Playbook Library

Stores validated repeatable market patterns.

Playbook requirements:

* Must be supported by repeated evidence.
* Must include conditions, invalidation rules, and risk limits.
* Must not override Constitution rules.
* Must not treat Stage 6 as tradable.

Required fields:

* Playbook name
* Market condition
* Trigger conditions
* Confirmation signals
* Invalidation signals
* Risk notes
* Historical examples
* Validation status

Status: structure defined, no formal playbooks promoted yet

---

## Lesson Library

Stores lessons from successful and failed decisions.

Required fields:

* Lesson date
* Related decision
* Expected outcome
* Actual outcome
* Root cause
* What the system missed
* What should change
* Whether model change is approved

Status: structure defined, RCA workflow pending

---

## Hypothesis Library

Stores ideas that are not yet allowed into the formal model.

Required fields:

* Hypothesis name
* Source
* Research owner
* Evidence summary
* Test method
* Required data
* Validation status
* Promotion criteria
* Rejection criteria

Rule:

No hypothesis becomes a permanent rule without validation and 小P approval.

Status: structure defined, active hypothesis registry pending

---

## Decision Log

Stores each Auto Radar decision with evidence and outcome tracking.

Required fields:

* Decision date
* Market mode
* Attack index
* Main theme
* Next theme
* Avoid themes
* Suggested exposure
* Entry condition
* Stop loss condition
* Take profit condition
* Confidence score
* Evidence summary
* Final action by 綠茶
* Outcome
* Review status

Status: structure defined, automated storage pending

---

## Pattern Evolution

Tracks how market reactions change over time.

Observed dimensions:

* Market Memory
* Expectation
* Awareness
* Information Diffusion
* AI Influence
* Pattern Saturation
* Adaptation

Required questions:

1. Has the market already learned this setup?
2. Did the reaction happen faster than before?
3. Did the market front-run the event?
4. Did good news fail to push price higher?
5. Did bad news fail to push price lower?
6. Should Theme Score, Stage Score, or Decision Score stay unchanged, enter hypothesis review, or be adjusted later?

Status: structure defined, scoring integration pending
